HealX Appointment Booking FAQs:

How can I book an appointment?
Patients can book appointments by visiting the Book Appointment screen, picking their hospital, select a department, choosing a time slot, and submitting. They can also use our medical chatbot to guide them.

What time slots are available?
Standard time slots are:
- Morning slots: 09:00 AM - 10:00 AM, 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM, 11:00 AM - 12:00 PM.
- Afternoon slots: 01:00 PM - 02:00 PM, 02:00 PM - 03:00 PM, 04:00 PM - 05:00 PM.

What happens if I miss my appointment slot?
If you are late or miss your slot, your queue position will be pushed down. If you do not show up, your appointment is marked as "no-show".

Can I cancel my appointment?
Yes, you can cancel appointments up to 1 hour before the slot. Cancelled appointments release the queue number, decreasing the waiting time for patients behind you.
