HealX Platform General Hospital Policies:

Emergency Guidelines:
In case of a severe medical emergency (heart attack, breathing issues, heavy bleeding, stroke, major accident), call the ambulance services immediately at 108. Do not wait for an online appointment or chatbot response.

First Aid Triage:
- Minor Cuts and Scrapes: Wash the wound with clean water, apply antiseptic cream, and cover with a sterile bandage.
- Fever: Keep hydrated, stay in a cool room, and consult a general physician if fever exceeds 101 degrees Fahrenheit for more than 2 days.
- Chest Pain: If accompanied by pain radiating to the left arm or jaw, sweating, and shortness of breath, treat as emergency immediately and go to the nearest emergency room.

Patient Admission Policy:
Admission requires a valid doctor prescription. Patients must present an Aadhar card or a valid ID card, along with health insurance card documents.
Discharge process is initiated by the attending doctor. Billing clearances must be obtained from the ground-floor billing counter prior to physical discharge.
